The infographic, titled "Navigating the Digital Frontier: A Student’s Guide," uses a structured, three-part layout to educate batchmates on digital essentials. The design features a notebook-style background with navy blue accents, creating an academic yet modern feel.

It addresses three core topics required by the module:

What is Digital Literacy? It provides a clear definition, emphasizing that it goes beyond hardware usage to include the evaluation and responsible communication of information.

Essential Tools: It lists high-impact productivity tools like Notion, Trello, and Google Scholar, helping students organize their academic life.

Professional Online Presence: It offers actionable advice on "auditing" one's digital footprint and maintaining a polished, consistent profile on platforms like LinkedIn.
